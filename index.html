Common questions

✈️ How do I calculate my travel budget?
Enter your main costs — transport, accommodation, food, activities. Switch to Expense Breakdown for more detail, or use Advanced Planner to map costs day by day. You can compare up to 3 different trip options side by side.
💶 How much does a trip to Europe cost?
A 7-day mid-range trip to Europe typically costs €800–€1,800 per person. Budget destinations like Prague or Budapest run €45–80/day, while Paris or Amsterdam average €150–200/day. Use the calculator with your specific destination to get a personalised estimate.
💾 Is my data saved automatically?
Yes — your scenarios are saved automatically in your browser. No account needed. Use the Share button to copy a link that preserves all your data, so you can return later or share it with travel companions.
